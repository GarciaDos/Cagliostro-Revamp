A Brief Overview

"Lupin III: Castle of Cagliostro" is a classic animated film directed by Hayao Miyazaki, originally released in 1979. It is the second feature film in the Lupin III franchise, which originated from the manga series created by Monkey Punch. The movie follows the exploits of the charismatic and roguish master thief, Lupin III, and his partner Jigen as they embark on a daring adventure to uncover the secrets of a counterfeit money operation in the small European country of Cagliostro.
